Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create an account?

Click the "Sign Up" button in the top navigation, fill out the registration form with your details, and verify your email address.

How do I start selling?

After creating an account, go to your profile and click "Create Listing" to add your products. Follow our seller guidelines for best results.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, debit cards, net banking, UPI, and digital wallets for secure transactions.

How long does shipping take?

Shipping times vary by seller and location. Most items are delivered within 3-7 business days within India.

Can I return products?

Yes! Most items can be returned within 30 days of delivery. Check the product page for specific return policies.

Is my personal information secure?

Absolutely. We use industry-standard encryption and security measures to protect your personal and financial information.
